About Us

YouthComputing is a non-profit organization committed to inspiring the future of technology, promoting the power of computer science, STEM, and innovation, as well as educating the leaders of tomorrow. We aim to use computer science to empower youth, advocate STEM, provide education and promote innovation throughout Alberta.
