More About Russell Thomas

Russell Thomas Business of the Arts

Bio:

Russell is a 20-year resident of Wood Buffalo who has always dabbled in the arts, as an actor, writer, director, producer and visual artist. He spent over a decade as the President of interPLAY, our community’s former visual and performing arts festival. He was also a one-term municipal councillor and served on the Premier’s Council for Culture for a number of years.

His shift into wild colour paintings began with a watercolour lesson from his father-in-law Neil in 2011. From that introduction to the medium, Russell began exploring the art of portraiture using unorthodox colour schemes. That experimentation set the stage for his move into acrylics in June 2014.

Among over 800 original works done in the last three years are a number of pieces that received local, regional, provincial and national attention. His tribute to Calgary theatre director Michael Green was featured in The Globe and Mail. Portraits of the four victims of the La Loche shootings made a significant impact in Saskatchewan. And the iconic portrait of Fire Chief Darby Allen made national headlines shortly after the evacuation of Fort McMurray and was seen by millions.

In addition to prolific commission work and workshops, Russell has had great success with raising funds for charitable causes through live paintings. The bulk of his creative work is done in Birdsong Studio, a repurposed workshop in his backyard.


More About Wendy Muise

Wendy Muise Technology and Entrepreneurship

Bio:

Wendy Muise is a diehard entrepreneur and strategist at heart. Using her passion in entrepreneurial strategy to foster Ground Floor Labs, her Alberta-based consulting firm. She is also the founder of Breast Buds, a Breast Cancer mobile app and is currently developing a Cannabis Workplace Impairment App. Wendy also works as the Business Facilitator for Business Link an Alberta based non-profit and works with entrepreneurs to identify and develop opportunities for entrepreneurial development. Wendy brings years of experience from the oil and gas industries, travel, banking, and startup scene. Having worked in several tech startups across Canada, Wendy has been responsible for everything from advising companies on product development, employee culture, operational strategies, business development, leadership, and growth. Wendy has helped small start-ups to develop technical products used by large retailers such as Home Depot, Neiman Marcus, and Walmart. She also relies on her education in business, which focused on strategy and leadership to drive economic change across the country. Wendy believes in contributing to the economic growth of her community one entrepreneur at a time. She spends countless hours working with hundreds of aspiring and seasoning entrepreneurs to help drive economic growth in Canada.


More About Barbara Weber

Barbara Weber Community and Technology

Bio:

Babs holds degrees in both archaeology (University of Calgary) and museum studies (University of Leicester). In the course of her adventures, she has done fieldwork in Jordan and Nicaragua; lived in an apartment converted from a hayloft once used by Land Army Girls on the estate where the richest Anglo Saxon burial ground was located; helped hundreds of children explore the Lethbridge river valley; taught senior citizens how to use iPads to explore historic photographs; and volunteered with many organizations like the Crowsnest Pass Adult Education Society, Girl Guides of Canada, Crowsnest Regional Library, FCSS and the Lethbridge Food Bank.

Through any job, she has always been very interested in how using technology effectively can help make the work easier, more creative or interactive, or better connected to the world. Technology isn’t the means to an end, it’s a tool with endless possibilities. At FuseSocial, Babs works with dozens of community agencies to help them find the tools they need to do their work. If an agency needs help with creating a business plan, policies, brainstorming ways to sell themselves, working on strategy or processes, brushing up their board members on how to be effective governors, or working towards national best practices then Babs is the Rosalind Russell to your Cary Grant: in other words, she’s your girl Friday!

Workshop summary:

Technology only exists because humans saw problems and built things to make their lives easier – sometimes with unexpected results. This workshop is going to explore ways that technology and innovation has totally changed the world and the way we interact with it over the last 2.5 million years, right up to today how computers and information technology has shaped our way of helping each other. We’ll then take a dive into human centered design and how that works with your projects today!


More About Melanie Galea

Melanie Galea Social Media Marketing

Bio:

Melanie Galea is a very proud Real Estate Professional for RE/MAX La Biche. She has been selling real estate in Fort McMurray for over 10 years and LOVING every minute of it. Her goal is this; she wants her clients experience to be personal, positive and exciting. She embraces technology and the power of social media and videography when it comes to the buying and selling processes.

The passion she has for Fort McMurray and small business has transpired into the Discover Fort McMurray app and Social media following. Over the last 2 years she has been interviewing and showcasing businesses, services and opportunities in the region in a video series which is posted online. Encouraging the community to discover, support and shop local.

Melanie sits on the Fort McMurray Chamber of Commerce board and is active with a variety of community organizations and groups. In her free time you can find her working on her golf swing; she has been a title sponsor last year and the Ladies Night sponsor with the Fort McMurray Golf Course for over 10 years.

Recently Melanie has started teaching social media and marketing seminars to local business owners. Sharing with them her insights, tools and techniques to effectively and efficiently market their business or service.


More About Luay Eljamal

Luay Eljamal Technology and Culture

More About D'Andre Wilson

D'Andre Wilson Design Project Pitch Prep

Bio:

D’Andre Wilson-Ihejirika is the founder, president and lead program consultant at BrainSTEM Alliance, a non-profit consulting company specializing in curriculum and program development in the areas of STEM (science, technology, engineering and math) as well as leadership and entrepreneurship. D’Andre was born and raised in Nassau, Bahamas, before moving to Canada to pursue a degree in Chemical Engineering at McGill University. D’Andre subsequently completed a Master’s Degree from the Centre for Management of Technology and Entrepreneurship at the University of Toronto before moving to Fort McMurray to pursue a career in the Oil Sands. D’Andre is also the co-founder of non-profit organization BETA (Bahamas Engineering and Technology Advancement) as well as a previous finalist in the Bahamas Ministry of Youth, Sports & Culture’s National Entrepreneurship Competition for her social enterprise Ziri Bags & Accessories. D’Andre has been the recipient of numerous awards and recognitions including the 2011 Centre for Social Innovation Agent of Change, 2012 GIrls Inc. Women of Inspiration Award, 2013 Alberta Venture Next 10 Influential People, 2013 Heart of Wood Buffalo Newcomer Engagement Award and the 2017 YMM Magazine Top 50 Under 50 Award.

D’Andre’s workshop will go through quick tips and tricks on putting together the key speaking points for your design project hackathon presentation to the judges, helping you highlight the key features of your project in the different criteria categories, including technology, community, arts and culture.


More About Scott Schellenberg

Scott Schellenberg SMS Mining Equipment

Bio:

Scott Schellenberg been in the mining equipment industry for over 20 years, and moved to Fort McMurray in 1996 with Transwest (now known as SMS Equipment) which is the Komatsu mining equipment dealer. Scott started his career with SMS as a mobile equipment electrician. Through the years he has held positions in technical support and operations management. Scott took on the next challenge in early 2015 as Sr. Manager, Autonomous Haulage and is now responsible for AHS business development as well as ongoing support for deployments in Canada. Over the past 20 years Scott has been part of a significant shift in the mining technology focus from mechanization to digitization.

Scott is planning to speak about the shift in focus from “mechanization” (Engineering bigger equipment to haul more) to digitization (how to maximize the use of the equipment to haul more though technology based solutions). He will also speak on the role of this technology in industry and about the technology behind today's Autonomous Haul Systems and some of the benefits it brings in safety and production. Finally, Scott will also talk about some of the roles this technology creates.

More About Dan Giurescu

Dan Giurescu Blockchain

Bio:

Dan is the Founder and CEO of TerraHub. Dan created TerraHub after his own attempt to build a blockchain product and struggling to find realistic, knowledgeable sources. Dan saw the need for a company to help cut through the myths and provide real world advice and guidance. Dan thrives on the challenge of commercializing new concepts, guiding teams and inspiring action by creating an environment where taking risks in pursuit of success is encouraged.
Dan leveraged his industry and regulatory knowledge, financial acumen and technology expertise to co-found Caledonia Solutions, a supply chain software for the dangerous goods industry, moving the industry away from paper and into digital innovation. Community focused - Dan supports numerous local charities and is a member in good standing with The A100 , the Alberta Rainforest Group and a Mentor with IBM District Ventures and Technovation International, a youth program for girls wanting to learn code.

Keynote description
Dan’s keynote will discuss the shift in the technology sector and how Blockchain and the fundamental software platforms will impact their future. He will speak to his work with Blockchain in application to Oil and Gas, Health and Wellness, Supply Chain Management, as well as his startup-career as an entrepreneur during and after University.
