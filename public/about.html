About Us

To use computer science as a means to promote youth STEM education and innovation.

Our Vision

To be a community computer science and STEM education organization that reaches youth across Alberta.

YouthComputing is a non-profit organization committed to inspiring the future of technology, promoting the power of computer science, STEM, and innovation, as well as educating the leaders of tomorrow. We aim to use computer science to empower youth, advocate STEM, provide education and promote innovation throughout Alberta. YouthComputing aims to achieve this by holding conferences, events, workshops, and competitions throughout Alberta to further emphasize the importance of computer science and STEM education.

YouthComputing consists of youth leaders with a strong passion for computer science. The core team of members are ready to promote youth involvement in computer science and STEM, as well as take on youth leadership roles to empower other youth. We want to inspire the future, promote the power of computer science and innovation, and educate the leaders of tomorrow.
